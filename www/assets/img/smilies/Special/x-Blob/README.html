Smellies  

Tired of those yellow smilies and boring emoticons? Here are the smellies, Kebawe's version of the smileys. Their design come from a creature appearing in Marko and Olier's French heroic-fantasy comic series: Agence Barbare. These little creatures can be really cute but unfortunately fart a lot.

Feel free to use the smellies on your IM software (ICQ, AIM, Jabber, MSN, AIM, etc.), or if you are a webmaster on your website, your forum, or your blog, but please credit us with a link to Kebawe.com. Thanks to the smellies' strong odor, we'll find those who didn't credit us and curse them forever (and even longer if we have enough magic points left).

You can find the latest smellies at http://kebawe.com/smellies/.
